Burger

What You Need to Cook a Burger

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef (80% lean/20% fat recommended)
  • Hamburger buns
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Cheese slices (cheddar, American, or your preference)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise

Equipment

  • Mixing bowl
  • Grill or skillet
  • Spatula
  • Knife
  • Cutting board

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, diced onion, minced garlic, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix gently with your hands until just combined (avoid overmixing).
  3. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape into patties about 1/2 inch thick. Make a slight depression in the center of each patty with your thumb (this prevents bulging during cooking).
  4. Preheat your grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  5. Cook the patties for about 3-4 minutes on each side for medium doneness, or adjust to your preferred level of doneness.
  6. If adding cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking.
  7. Toast the hamburger buns lightly if desired.
  8. Assemble your burger: bottom bun, condiments, lettuce, burger patty with cheese, tomato, pickles, more condiments if desired, and top bun.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy!
