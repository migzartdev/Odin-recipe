Pizza

What You Need to Cook a Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Optional toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, peppers, onions, etc.)

Equipment

  • Oven
  • Baking tray or pizza stone
  • Rolling pin (optional)
  • Mixing bowl
  • Knife
  • Spoon or ladle

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Add your desired toppings.
  6. Drizzle a little olive oil and sprinkle salt.
  7. Transfer the pizza to a baking tray or pizza stone.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes, until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  9. Remove from oven, slice, and enjoy!
